Core Features

Everything your job search needs.

Built by job seekers, for job seekers. Every feature designed around how the search actually works.

📋
Applications Table

Track every role, company, status, and stage in one clean table. Filter by role type, industry, location, and status. Search everything instantly.

📊
Analytics Dashboard

Visualise your pipeline with conversion rates, role type breakdowns, industry charts, and daily activity. Know exactly where you stand.

🌊
Sankey Flow Chart

See your entire application journey visualised — from applied to offer. Watch where applications drop off at each stage in real time.

📝
Status Timeline

Log every stage per application — phone screens, interviews, offers. Each job has its own timeline that powers the flow chart automatically.

📤
Export & Import

Export your full dataset as JSON anytime. Import it on any browser, any device. Your data is always yours — no lock-in.

⚙️
Zero Setup

Open the app and start tracking. No account, no signup, no subscription. Data lives in your browser until you export it.

AI-Powered

Claude works with you.

Bring your own Anthropic API key and unlock three AI-powered features that go beyond simple tracking.

✦ AI
Auto Import

Paste a job posting URL or description. Claude reads it, extracts the title, company, location, salary, role type, and industry — and adds it to your tracker in one click.

✦ AI
Resume Review

Paste your resume and a job description. Get a match score, strengths, gaps, missing keywords, and specific recommendations to improve your fit.

✦ AI
Offer Comparison

Select two or three offers from your tracker. Claude scores each on compensation, growth potential, and lifestyle fit — then recommends the best choice.

How it works

Simple by design.

No tutorial needed. Open the app and you'll know what to do.

01
Add your applications

Manually add jobs or use Auto Import to paste a URL — Claude extracts all the details automatically.

02
Log your progress

As you hear back, add stages to each job's timeline — phone screen, interviews, offer. The flow chart updates automatically.

03
Analyse and decide

Review your analytics, compare offers side by side, and use Resume Review to prep for every interview.
